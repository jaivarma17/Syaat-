In the pursuit of a unified vision, it is our endeavor to consolidate the noble mission of Yog Yogeshwar Ramlal Ji Bhagwan, Yog Yogeshwar Mulakh Raj Ji Bhagwan, Yog Yogeshwar DeviDayal Ji Mahadev under a single distinguished banner. We undertake the stewardship of Yog Sadhna Ashrams, Yog Abhyas Ashrams, Divya Yog Mandirs, Yog Divya Mandirs, and Yog Chikitsalyas established by the revered Yogeshwar Ramlal Bhagwan, Yogeshwar Mulakh Raj Bhagwan, and Yogeshwar DeviDayal Mahadev, ensuring the seamless management of their regular affairs. Our paramount goal is to advance and disseminate the principles and practices of Yoga, fostering widespread access to proper Yogic education among the masses. We strive to elevate societal character through the establishment of Yoga schools and colleges, promoting a holistic approach to well-being.

Respecting the sanctity and integrity of the philosophy of Yoga, we maintain a comprehensive library of Yoga literature and cultivate camaraderie among adherents of Yogic principles. Through scholarly publications and research endeavours, we endeavor to deepen the understanding and appreciation of Yoga.

We extend our reach far and wide by establishing branches and organizing camps across India and beyond, overseeing their operations and facilitating demonstrations of Yogic techniques. Furthermore, we dedicate ourselves to the creation of sacred spaces for the veneration of Yog Yogeshwar Mahaprabhu RamLal Ji Bhagwan, Yog Yogeshwar MulakhRaj Ji Bhagwan, Yog Yogeshwar DeviDayal Ji Mahadev & Yog Yogeshwar Surender Dev Ji Mahadev, commemorating their birth, enlightenment, and other significant occasions to nurture spiritual growth.

In our commitment to holistic healing, we train Yogacharyas in the art of alleviating human ailments and fostering inner peace through Yogic practices, herbal remedies, and the comprehensive Ashtang Yoga system. Recognizing their expertise, we confer degrees upon these accomplished Yogacharyas under the auspices of Shri Sadgurudev Ji and Pradhan Yogacharya Dev.

We also extend our support to charitable endeavors, including the establishment of institutions and old age homes for the benefit of our members and those engaged in the pursuit of Yoga. Embracing philanthropy, we gratefully accept gifts, endowments, and bequests aligned with our mission, ensuring their judicious use in accordance with lawful trusts.

Prudently managing our resources, we invest funds solely towards the fulfillment and enhancement of our objectives, including the acquisition of properties sanctioned by Shri Sadgurudev Ji and Pradhan Yogacharya Dev. Upholding transparency and integrity, we affirm that all income and assets of the Federation are dedicated solely to the promotion of our stated aims, with no provision for personal gain by any member, past or present.
