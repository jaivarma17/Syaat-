Mahaprabhu Ji’s Yoga Camp

Get ready to embark on this endless journey within you! With the blessings of Ramlal Mulakh Raj DeviDayal Surender Dev Ji Maharaj Yog Abhyas Ashram Society (Regd.) organises Mahaprabhu Ji’s yoga camp from 24th to 26th June, 2017 at Chhatrasal Stadium, Delhi. A yoga movement for the sole purpose of sharing love, compassion and peace with all fellow devotees, to cure ailments and heal mind, body & soul. Join our Mahaprabhu Ji’s yoga camp on the magical path of Yoga. Under the Sublime Guidance of Grand Son of Yogeshwar DeviDayal Ji Maharaj & Son of Swami Surender Dev Ji Maharaj Yogacharya Amit Dev. The event is open to all, devoid of age, gender and religion.
