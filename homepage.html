Welcome to Shri Yog Abhyas Ashram Trust [Regd.], a venerable institution with roots tracing back to 1888, when the First Guru Gaddi Yogeshwar Ramlal Bhagwan laid its foundation. Committed to the timeless principles of Yog, our non-profit organization has evolved into a beacon of tranquility and wellness, dedicated to advancing and disseminating Yog practices worldwide.

At the heart of our mission lies a profound belief in the transformative power of Yog as a holistic remedy for human ailments. Embracing Yog as a scientific solution, we strive to integrate its principles into the fabric of everyday life. With a vision to establish Yogic colleges and schools for the betterment of society, we tirelessly pursue avenues for research, publication, and education.

Our impact extends far beyond the confines of our ashram, as we actively host camps both within India and abroad, spreading the ethos of Yog to every corner of the globe. Through these immersive experiences, we guide participants of all ages in the practice of Yogic Kriyas, Asanas, Pranayamas, and Mudras, nurturing not only physical well-being but also inner harmony.

Today, the SYAAT International Yogic Cure and Research Center stands as a testament to our dedication, with 32 locations nationwide serving as hubs of healing and enlightenment. Join us on this journey towards a healthier, more harmonious world, where the wisdom of Yog illuminates every path.
